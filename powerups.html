There are different powerups that you can gain throughout the game, this page explains what each different one does.

  • Green: Size Down
  • You size goes down, which allows you to dodge Asteroids easier. This also adds 5 to your score.
  • Blue: Invulnerability
  • You gain invulnerability for 5 seconds, allowing you to increase your score greatly. This also adds 5 to your score.
  • Red: Huge Score
  • Your score is multiplied by 3, and your size does not increase.
